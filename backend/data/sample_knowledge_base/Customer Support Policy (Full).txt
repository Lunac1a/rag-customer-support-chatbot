1. Order Processing

All orders placed through our online store are processed during standard business hours, Monday to Friday, excluding public holidays. Orders are typically processed within 1–2 business days after payment confirmation.

Once an order is processed, customers will receive an order confirmation email followed by a shipping notification email containing tracking information (if applicable). Processing times may be longer during peak seasons, major sales events, or public holidays.

If a customer notices an error in their order (such as incorrect shipping address or item selection), they should contact customer support as soon as possible. Changes can only be made before the order enters the shipping stage.

Orders that have already been shipped cannot be modified, but customers may still be eligible for return or exchange according to the return policy described below.

2. Shipping Policy

We offer several shipping options depending on the customer’s location and preferred delivery speed.

Standard Shipping

Estimated delivery time: 3–7 business days

Available for most domestic orders

Tracking is provided for all standard shipments

Express Shipping

Estimated delivery time: 1–3 business days

Available in selected regions

Higher shipping fees apply

International Shipping

Estimated delivery time: 7–21 business days

Delivery times may vary depending on customs clearance and destination country regulations

Customers are responsible for any applicable customs duties or import taxes

Once an order is shipped, the customer will receive a tracking number via email. Tracking updates may take up to 24 hours to appear in the courier’s system.

If a package appears to be delayed or stuck in transit for more than 5 business days beyond the estimated delivery window, customers are encouraged to contact support for assistance.

3. Returns and Refunds

We offer a 30-day return policy for most products purchased through our store.

Customers may return an item if:

The item is unused and in original condition

The item is returned within 30 days of delivery

The original packaging is included

Items that cannot be returned include:

Gift cards

Digital products

Items marked as final sale

To initiate a return, customers should contact customer support and provide their order number along with the reason for the return.

Once the returned item is received and inspected, a refund will be issued to the original payment method. Refund processing typically takes 3–5 business days, although the exact time may depend on the customer's bank or payment provider.

Shipping costs are non-refundable, unless the return is due to a mistake on our part (for example, receiving the wrong item).

4. Exchanges

Customers may request an exchange if they receive:

A damaged item

An incorrect item

A defective product

Exchange requests must be submitted within 7 days of delivery.

In such cases, the company will cover the cost of return shipping and send a replacement item at no additional cost to the customer.

If the replacement item is unavailable, customers may choose between:

A full refund

Store credit

Waiting for the item to be restocked

Customers should include photos of the damaged or incorrect item when contacting support, as this helps speed up the exchange process.

5. Order Cancellation

Orders can be cancelled within 12 hours of purchase without any penalty.

After 12 hours, cancellation requests may not be guaranteed because the order may already be processed or shipped.

If a customer wishes to cancel an order after the processing stage, the cancellation may be declined, and the customer will need to follow the return procedure after the item is delivered.

6. Warranty Policy

Some products include a limited warranty covering manufacturing defects.

Warranty coverage typically lasts 12 months from the date of delivery, unless otherwise specified on the product page.

The warranty does not cover:

Damage caused by misuse or improper handling

Accidental damage

Normal wear and tear

Unauthorized repairs or modifications

To file a warranty claim, customers must provide:

Proof of purchase

A description of the issue

Photos or videos showing the defect

Customer support will review the claim and may offer repair, replacement, or refund depending on the situation.
